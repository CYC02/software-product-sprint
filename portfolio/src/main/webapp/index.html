About

Hello! I'm currently a rising junior at Hunter College in New York City. I love drawing, digital art, and gaming! My family immigrated to the US from China, and I was born and raised in NYC. My family speaks Taishanese and Cantonese, but I'm not fluent with either dialect. I'm currently learning Chinese (Mandarin) in college; so I can improve my understanding of the language. When I was younger, I would watch anime on the weekends like YuGiOh and Dragon Ball Z , and I think my interest in drawing and eventually digital art started from watching those shows. I also played Pokemon games and Sonic games on the Gameboy which led to my interest in game development. One day, I would like to create a game where people can connect over it and have fun.

Projects

Winter 2021 Hunter Codefest Submission

Hunter Codefest Project

In the winter of 2020-2021, there was a college event called the Hunter 2021 Codefest. The students would form their groups on Slack, and I teamed up with five other students. We created a website with a game called "A Lockdown Story." We finished this project in about six weeks and presented it to Google engineers. At this time, I was the only freshman in my group. So I was very inexperienced, and I focused on producing the art in the game. There were many things we wanted to add to the game, but we couldn't due to time constraints. Regardless, I like how this project came out, so if you'd like, take a look!


Gender in Gaming | WGS 100 Final Project

Final Project for WGS 100

The main focus of this project is the animation, but I decided to put it on a website because I would have a place to write additional ideas about my topic. I created this as a final project for my WGS 100 class. I spent about 2-3 weeks on this project, and I focused on the animation in the first two weeks. I used the knowledge I gained from the Hunter Codefest to create a simple website hosted on Github. If I had more time, I would've added more to the website and made the animation smoother.
